Case Study: ICT in a Manufacturing Industry

Home What is ICT? ICT in Industry Benefits Case Study Survey Team Contact

Background

A local manufacturing factory was facing issues such as low productivity, manual record keeping, and frequent errors in production planning.

Problems Before ICT

  • Manual inventory management
  • Production delays
  • Lack of real-time data
  • Higher operational costs

ICT Solutions Implemented

  • ERP system for inventory and planning
  • IoT sensors for machine monitoring
  • Automation in production processes
  • Digital reporting system

Results After ICT

  • Improved production efficiency
  • Reduction in errors
  • Better resource management
  • Faster decision making
