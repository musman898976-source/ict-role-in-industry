Use of ICT in Modern Industry

ICT plays a vital role in modern industries by improving efficiency, automation, communication, and decision-making processes.

Key ICT Applications in Industry

1. Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)

ERP systems help industries manage resources such as inventory, finance, human resources, and production planning in a centralized system.

2. Internet of Things (IoT)

IoT uses sensors and connected devices to monitor machines, temperature, pressure, and production in real time.

3. Automation & Control Systems

Automation reduces manual work by using PLCs, robots, and automated machines to improve accuracy and speed.

4. CAD / CAM Systems

Computer-Aided Design (CAD) and Computer-Aided Manufacturing (CAM) help engineers design and manufacture products with high precision.

Impact of ICT on Industrial Performance

  • Faster production
  • Reduced errors
  • Better quality control
  • Improved communication
  • Data-driven decisions
