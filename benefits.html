Key Benefits of ICT

The integration of ICT in industry provides several advantages that improve productivity, quality, and overall business performance.

Without ICT With ICT
Manual record keeping Digital data management
Slow communication Real-time communication
High chances of error Reduced human errors
Low production efficiency High efficiency and automation
Delayed decision making Data-driven decision making
