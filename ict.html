Definition of ICT

Information and Communication Technology (ICT) refers to the use of digital technologies such as computers, software, networks, and communication systems to store, process, and exchange information efficiently.

Examples of ICT

  • Computers and Servers
  • Internet and Networking Systems
  • Software Applications
  • Cloud Computing
  • Mobile Communication

Why ICT is Important?

ICT improves speed, accuracy, and communication. It helps organizations and industries make better decisions, reduce manual work, and increase productivity.
