Purpose of Survey

The survey was conducted to understand awareness about the role of ICT in industry among students and young professionals.

Survey Method

  • Platform: Google Forms
  • Participants: Students and local industry workers
  • Total Responses: 30 (sample)

Key Findings

  • 80% respondents believe ICT improves productivity
  • 70% agreed ICT reduces human errors
  • 60% think ICT skills are essential for engineers
  • Most participants wanted more training on industrial ICT

Conclusion

The survey results show that ICT awareness is important and there is a strong need to educate students and industry workers about modern ICT tools.
